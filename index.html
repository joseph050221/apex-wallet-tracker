Upload a CSV or PDF bank/credit-card statement. By default, everything is parsed right here in your browser — nothing is uploaded anywhere. Help & Tutorial

            
          


          

            
A quick rundown of everything ApexWallet Tracker can do. Reopen this anytime from the Help button in the sidebar.


            

              
Adding Your First Card

              
Go to My Cards → Add New Card. Give it a name, brand, last 4 digits, and optionally a credit limit to track usage with a progress bar. Click any card afterward to edit or delete it.

            


            

              
Logging an Expense

              
Use the Add Expense button in the top bar to log a purchase manually. Every transaction appears in the ledger below, where you can edit or delete it anytime.

            


            

              
Importing a Bank Statement

              
Click Import, choose which card the statement belongs to, then upload a .csv or .pdf file. Everything is parsed right in your browser by default — nothing is uploaded anywhere unless you turn on AI parsing (below).

            


            

              
AI-Powered PDF Parsing (Optional)

              
If a PDF's layout trips up the built-in parser, add your own Anthropic API key in Profile & Settings → AI-Powered PDF Import — get one at console.anthropic.com/settings/keys, billed a fraction of a cent per statement directly to your own account. Then click Try AI-Powered Parsing in the Import dialog.

            


            

              
Logging a Card Payment

              
On any card in My Cards, click the payment icon to record a payment toward that card's bill. It counts toward your total spending for accuracy, but doesn't reduce the card's tracked usage.

            


            

              
Personal / Business / All

              
The switcher at the top of the page filters the Dashboard, My Cards, Analytics, and the ledger by scope. Assign a card as Personal or Business when you add or edit it.

            


            

              
Analytics & Trends

              
The Spending Trend chart supports Week, Month, 3M, 6M, and Year ranges, alongside a Card Spending Distribution chart and a detailed category breakdown.

            


            

              
Monthly Report

              
In Analytics, click Generate Monthly Report for a consolidated report across every card in view, with each transaction attributed to its card. Print or save it as a PDF, or opt into getting it emailed automatically each month from Profile & Settings.

            


            

              
Category Budgets & Alerts

              
Set personal and business budgets per category in Profile & Settings to trigger spending alerts, and enable desktop notifications there too.
